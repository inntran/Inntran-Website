Hardening & Optimization - 安全加固与优化

Basic services hardening - 基本服务加固

netservices limited will disable remote access of some services, like dtlogin

Disable Unused Services - 禁用不需要的服务

Most servers do not use "Solaris Management Console", nor "Web Console"
大部分服务器不需要诸如“Solaris管理控制台”或者“Web控制台”

To shutdown these two Java application
要关闭这两个Java的应用程序

svcadm disable wbem webconsole

To disable the GUI
要禁用图形用户界面

svcadm disable cde-login ogl-select fc-cache stosreg autoreg cde-calendar-manager stfsloader cde-printinfo smserver

Hardening SSH - 加固SSH

如果要开启Root用户的SSH远程连接，需要改动 /etc/ssh/sshd_config 中的如 下参数

PermitRootLogin 'no' -> 'yes'
